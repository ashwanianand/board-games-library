## Overview

Mafia is a classic social deduction party game. One group of players (the Mafia) secretly knows each other's identities, while the larger group of Townspeople tries to identify and eliminate them by majority vote. Each night the Mafia eliminates a Townsperson; each day the town deliberates and votes to eliminate a suspect. The game ends when all Mafia are eliminated or the Mafia equals or outnumbers the Town.
